How to: Impress the CEO

by Elan Fraiman

Posted on June 4, 2018 at 1:21 AM

You got to the interview. Cool. But now what? 3 Tips to make sure the CEO can't look past you.

1. Give the guy some sunlight. He's cold blooded so he starts his day with some warming-up. Dont stare, just keep walking while casually saying "Hi"

2. Food. He wants food. Everything you dont want, he wants it. Unless you're vegan, than you might need to share. Cockroaches, ants, crickets and maybe some plants.

“He viewed us, as we passed him by, With calm and yet with questioning eye, But moveless still, as though the stone Were portion of his being's own.”

Edward Robeson Taylor www.goodreads.com

3. Don't compare your CEO to a Komodo Dragon. That's sensitive. You don't compare your girlfriend out loud with Angelina Jolie right? Nope. You get it.
